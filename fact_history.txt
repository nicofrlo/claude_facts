2026-05-05: Cleopatra lived closer in time to the Moon landing (1969 CE) than to the construction of the Great Pyramid of Giza (~2560 BCE). Born around 69 BCE, she was separated from the pyramids by ~2,491 years, but only ~2,038 years from the Moon landing.
2026-05-06: Venus rotates so slowly on its axis that a single Venusian day (243 Earth days) is actually longer than a Venusian year (225 Earth days) — meaning on Venus, your birthday would arrive before a full day ever passed, and the sun rises in the west and sets in the east.
